Here are some definitions used:

  • CV: the coefficient of variation
  • DOC: the sequencing depth (Depth of Coverage), that is the avearge of the genome coverage shown here above
  • MAD: median of the absolute median deviation median(|X-median(X)|)
  • std: standard deviation
